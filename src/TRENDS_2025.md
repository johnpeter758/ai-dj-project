# Music Trends 2025-2026

## Hot on TikTok
- Lo-fi hip-hop beats
- Remix culture (sped up/slowed)
- Country crossover
- 15-30 second viral hooks

## Spotify Charts
- Afrobeats/Afro-fusion growth
- Regional Mexican (Peso Pluma)
- Pop-punk revival
- Album listening up

## EDM/Festival Sounds
- Hyperpop/Glitchcore
- Hard techno
- Phonk (Drumcode era)
- Bass-heavy house
- Live jam electronics

## Hip-Hop/Trap
- Drill evolution
- Trap-soul (R&B x trap)
- Triplet flows
- 808s with reverb tails

## Viral Production
- Drop before the drop
- Sampled "ahs" and "ohs"
- Sidechain compression
- Mid-side EQ
- TikTok-friendly intros

## Emerging Genres
- Phonk
- Hyperpop
- Amapiano
- Corridos tumbados
- Cloud rap
- Drift phonk

## What Goes Viral
1. Instant hook (0-5 sec)
2. 15-sec extractability
3. Dance/trend potential
4. Emotional resonance
5. Relatable lyrics
6. Remix potential

## Production Tips
- Sidechain to kick
- Wide stereo on synths
- Lo-fi saturation
- Quick punchy intros
- Short verses (8-16 bars)
- Big simple drops
