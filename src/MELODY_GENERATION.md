# Melody Generation

## Algorithms
- Markov Chains
- LSTM/GRU
- Transformer Models
- VAE
- GANs

## Hook Creation
- Repetition (2-4 bars, 2-4 times)
- Simple contour (rising/falling/arch)
- Rhythmic signature
- Pitch anchoring

## Motif Development
- Sequence (repeat at different pitch)
- Inversion (flip intervals)
- Retrograde (reverse)
- Augmentation/Diminution

## Melody-Harmony
- Chord tone targeting
- Non-harmonic tones
- Voice leading

## Memorability
- Frequent pitch repetition
- Simple intervals
- Rhythmic simplicity
- Tonal clarity
