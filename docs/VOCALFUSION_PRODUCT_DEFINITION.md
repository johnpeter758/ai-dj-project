# VocalFusion Product Definition (Phase 1)

## What VocalFusion **is**

VocalFusion is an AI music system that turns two parent songs into one intentional child arrangement that reads as a real song, not a stitched medley.

It combines analysis, planning, and rendering to produce a coherent full-track structure with deliberate section-level decisions.

## What VocalFusion **is not**

- Not an autoplay DJ crossfade tool.
- Not a random stem blender.
- Not a sequence of disconnected mashup moments.
- Not a medley where songs simply take turns.

## Permanent design rules

1. **At any point in time, only musically necessary elements should be active.**
2. **Every section must have a purpose: setup, build, tension, release, swap, peak, or outro.**
